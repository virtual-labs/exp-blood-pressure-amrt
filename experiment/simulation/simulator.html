Measurement of Blood Pressure (Sphygmomanometer)

Instructions

  1. Click on Back to Theory to go back to the theory page.
  2. Click on the Reset button to reset the experiment.
  3. Click on the Start Experiment button to start the experiment.
  4. Select the patient BP condition from the dropdown menu.
  5. Click on the Select Cuff button and then click the cuff to place it on the patient's arm.
  6. Click on the Select Stethoscope button and then click the stethoscope to begin auscultation.
  7. Hold the Pump (Inflate) button to increase the pressure above the systolic level.
  8. Slowly press the Valve (Deflate) button to reduce the pressure gradually.
  9. Observe when the heartbeat starts (systolic pressure detected).
  10. Observe when the heartbeat stops (diastolic pressure detected).
  11. View the detected values in the measurement log.
