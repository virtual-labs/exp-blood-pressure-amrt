Measurement of Blood Pressure (Sphygmomanometer)

Instructions

  1. Select a BP condition from the dropdown.
  2. Click the Sphygmomanometer (cuff) to place it on the patient’s arm.
  3. Click the Stethoscope to begin auscultation.
  4. Hold the PUMP (Inflate) button to increase pressure above systolic level.
  5. Slowly press the VALVE (Deflate) button to reduce pressure gradually.
  6. Observe when the heartbeat starts (systolic pressure detected).
  7. Observe when the heartbeat stops (diastolic pressure detected).
  8. View detected values in the measurement log.
